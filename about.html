One Music Square

As a local guitar and music shop located in Huntsville, TX, One Music Square has become a one-stop shop for anything and everything music. From vintage guitars and amps, to limited edition instruments, music books and even pianos and keyboards, if there is an instrument you need, One Music Square is sure to have it. The owner, Chip Looney worked at the Houston based H&H Music Company which is where he learned to repair, set up and sell instruments. After working for the musical instrument dealer for a number of years, Looney began working at the Montgomery County Music Center in Conroe.

For more information about how to sign-up for music lessons, call Chip Looney at (936) 295-3819.
